Fuels and Services

World class and reliable, Bharat Petroleum has built trust amongst its customers across the country with quality products and continues to do so since its inception 60 years ago. Now 18,622 Fuel Stations strong, the brand has thrived in the industry with its Pure for Sure promise.


Bharat Petroleum has a variety of product offerings like Petrol, Diesel, Automotive LPG and CNG along with premium petrol products like Speed and Speed 97 that guarantee a better experience for driving enthusiasts. But the brand goes above and beyond these offerings to meet the needs of a modern world. This includes specialized Fuel Station formats like Ghar, Highway Star, Pure for Sure, and other such services that guarantee a smooth business experience.


While consistent product quality is what ascertains customer-trust, what makes the brand stand out are its relevant and specialized business solutions. Today, 14,856 Fuel Stations are automated and the number continues to rise. These Fuel Stations enable easy commute experience for all travelers with stores like In & Out which are equipped with Restaurants, Convenience Stores, ATMs, a one-stop-shop for commuters across the country.


Convenience being at the core of what the brand stands for, Bharat Petroleum has also come up with SmartDrive - a perfect app for the tech-savvy traveler. The app lets users locate the nearest fuel stations and so much more in just a few clicks. Moreover, the brand offers its customers with loyalty programmes like PetroBonus and SmartFleet as well, which focus on the needs of motorists, transporters and commercial vehicle owners alike.


Showing love and care towards the planet has always been the norm for Bharat Petroleum. Its Fuel Stations, automated as well as others, are equipped with solar technologies to promote use of clean and green energy. At the same time, the brand also adheres to strict parameters by ensuring it serves only fuel-efficient vehicles, which in turn saves fuel.


Quality products, impeccable services and undoubted convenience at every fuel station drives Bharat Petroleum further towards delivering the Pure for Sure promise.
