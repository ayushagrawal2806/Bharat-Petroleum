Transforming Ideas into Innovation

Companies worldwide face a business environment presenting unprecedented challenges. Resource constraint is a norm; market conditions are complex, fast paced, volatile and the competition is unforgiving. In these circumstances it is a truism that innovation through customer-centric R&D will demarcate the winners from the also-rans.


For Bharat Petroleum, fueling the dreams of the nation means nurturing and transforming “fertile” ideas into “mature” innovations. Moving with the corporate mission to apply best technologies and always being the first choice of customers, distinct efforts are being made towards R&D.


At the pivot of this vigorous quest is Corporate Research & Development Centre (CRDC). It is a dynamic community of scientists and engineers, having experience and exposure in multiple domains of science and technology, to take innovation to newer heights.
