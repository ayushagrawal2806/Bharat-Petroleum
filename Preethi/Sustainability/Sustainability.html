A Cause for Sustainability

We at Bharat Petroleum are thoroughly aware that by virtue of the sector we belong to, we are perceived as a potential threat to the sustainability of resources, the environment and society. This perception is what makes our role in the country more critical.


It is important for us to incorporate sustainable practices for each of our key sustainability development issues with defined targets and indicators. Our priority is to enhance energy and operational efficiency, improve processes and technologies; thereby allowing us to minimise our impact on the environment and subsequently varied stakeholders.


Creating a healthy, safe and secure workplace has always been at the top of our priority list and we are constantly improving and exploring systems and practices towards achieving this goal.
