RTI in Bharat Petroleum

Indian Citizens have tasted the flavour of RTI Act during last 17 years since its inception. And in these 17 years, we have answered more than 49,600 RTI Queries, 7050 Appeals and more than 1150 second Appeals to CIC as a testimony, that at BPCL, RTI Act is followed with true spirit.

This page is for seeking information through an RTI.
