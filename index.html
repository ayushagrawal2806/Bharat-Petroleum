Fuel & Services

Fuel & Services

Bharat Petroleum is committed towards serving its customers in the best possible way by offering customer-friendly Fuel Stations across the length and breadth of the country.

Bharatgas

Bharatgas

Bharat Petroleum’s flagship product Bharatgas has been dominating the LPG gas market since the past three decades reaching out to over 80 million homes.

MAK Lubricants

MAK Lubricants

Bharat Petroleum understands the importance of the right lubricants in extending the life of vehicles, and offers a complete range of quality products across categories such as Automotive Engine Oils, Gear Oils, Transmission Oils and Specialty Oils.

Refineries

Refineries

Bharat Petroleum is committed towards serving its customers in the best possible way by offering customer-friendly Fuel Stations across the length and breadth of the country.

Gas

Gas

Bharat Petroleum has expanded its fuel portfolio to include natural gas, with an objective of providing energy security to millions of Indians.

Industrial & Commercial

Industrial & Commercial

At Bharat Petroleum, we cater to the fuel requirements of around 8000 industries, across the Public & Private sectors and various Govt. establishments such as Defense, Railways, State Transport Undertakings, State Electricity Boards etc.

International Trade

International Trade

At BPCL, International Trade involves the import of crude oil, import and export of finished products, transportation of coastal product, risk management.

Proficiency Testing

Proficiency Testing

To cater to the needs of petroleum sector laboratories in and around India, Bharat Petroleum has launched Proficiency Testing labs.
